About the Dataset

📋

Source & Publisher

Published by the World Bank via the Debtor Reporting System (DRS). Countries that borrow from the World Bank or IMF are required to report their public and publicly-guaranteed external debt annually.

World Bank DRS Open Data Annual
🗂️

Coverage & Scope

Covers long-term public & publicly guaranteed debt, private non-guaranteed debt, and short-term debt. Includes debt stocks (balance), flows (disbursements & repayments), and ratios (debt/GNI, debt service/exports).

Low-income Middle-income 1970–present

Data Categories & Pages

6 major indicator groups

🏛️

External Debt Stocks

Total outstanding debt owed to foreign creditors at year-end. Covers long-term public & publicly guaranteed (PPG), private non-guaranteed (PNG), short-term debt, and IMF credit. The foundational measure of a country's debt burden.

DT.DOD.DECT.CD PPG PNG Short-term
💸

Debt Flows & Disbursements

Tracks new borrowing (disbursements) and repayments (principal + interest) each year. Allows analysis of net flows — whether a country is a net receiver or net payer of debt financing. Key for understanding debt sustainability.

DT.DIS.DECT.CD DT.AMT.DECT.CD DT.INT.DECT.CD
📊

Debt Ratios & Key Indicators

Analytical ratios used to assess debt sustainability: Debt-to-GNI, Debt Service-to-Exports, Interest-to-Exports, Short-term Debt as % of reserves. These ratios are featured prominently in the International Debt Report (IDR) publication.

Debt/GNI % DS/Exports % Reserves
🌐

Creditor Composition

Breakdown of who holds a country's debt — multilateral institutions (World Bank, IMF, regional development banks), bilateral creditors (G7, Paris Club, China), private bondholders, and commercial banks. Critical for restructuring analysis.

Multilateral Bilateral Bondholders Commercial
📅

Debt Service Projections

Future scheduled principal and interest payments. Shows upcoming debt service obligations for each country — essential for assessing liquidity risk and near-term financing needs. Also includes DSSI (Debt Service Suspension Initiative) data.

Future payments DSSI Liquidity risk
🏷️

Country & Income Metadata

Country classification by income group (low, lower-middle, upper-middle), regional grouping, and DRS reporting status (A=as reported, P=preliminary, E=estimated). Enables comparative regional and income-group analysis.

ISO codes Income groups Regions DRS status

Key Indicators Reference

Most commonly used IDS indicators

DT.DOD.DECT.CD

External debt stocks, total

Total outstanding external debt at year-end. Includes long-term public, private, and short-term debt plus IMF credit. Unit: current USD.

DT.DIS.DECT.CD

Disbursements on external debt, total

New external debt borrowed during the year. Includes PPG long-term, PNG long-term disbursements. Unit: current USD.

DT.AMT.DECT.CD

Principal repayments on external debt

Principal payments made on long-term external debt during the year. Excludes short-term and IMF repayments. Unit: current USD.

DT.INT.DECT.CD

Interest payments on external debt

Interest paid on all external debt during the year. Covers both long-term and short-term instruments. Unit: current USD.

DT.TDS.DECT.GN.ZS

Debt service (% of GNI)

Total debt service (principal + interest) as a percentage of gross national income. Key sustainability ratio used by IMF/World Bank DSA frameworks.

DT.TDS.DECT.EX.ZS

Debt service (% of exports)

Total debt service as a percentage of goods, services, and primary income exports. Critical liquidity indicator — above 20% signals stress.

DT.DOD.DECT.GN.ZS

External debt stocks (% of GNI)

Total external debt stock as a share of gross national income. IMF sustainability benchmark: above 150% for low-income countries indicates high risk.

DT.DOD.MWBG.CD

PPG multilateral World Bank debt

Outstanding public & publicly guaranteed debt owed specifically to the World Bank Group (IBRD + IDA). Unit: current USD.

DT.DOD.BLAT.CD

PPG bilateral debt stocks

Public debt owed to official bilateral creditors (governments) such as Paris Club members, China's Export-Import Bank, and other sovereign lenders.

DT.DOD.BONS.CD

PPG bonds

Public debt issued as bonds on international capital markets. Includes Eurobonds and other traded fixed-income instruments issued by sovereigns.

DT.DOD.PRVS.CD

Private nonguaranteed debt

External debt owed by the private sector without government guarantee. Includes corporate bonds, bank loans, and other private borrowings from abroad.

DT.DOD.DSTC.CD

Short-term external debt

External debt with original maturity of one year or less. High short-term debt relative to reserves is a key early-warning indicator of balance of payments crises.

Methodology Notes

DRS Reporting Status

Countries are classified as (A) As Reported, (P) Preliminary, or (E) Estimated. "Estimated" data involves significant World Bank staff estimation due to incomplete country reporting.

Currency & Valuation

All data reported in current USD. Cross-currency debt is converted using end-of-year exchange rates. SDR-denominated IMF credit uses IMF official SDR/USD exchange rates.

HIPC & Debt Relief

Data includes the effects of HIPC (Heavily Indebted Poor Countries) and MDRI (Multilateral Debt Relief Initiative) debt relief. Pre-relief figures available in notes.

Data sourced from the World Bank International Debt Statistics dataset on Kaggle and the World Bank Open Data API. Dashboard built for educational and analytical purposes.
